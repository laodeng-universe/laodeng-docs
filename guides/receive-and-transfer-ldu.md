# Receive and Transfer LDU

This guide explains how to receive and transfer LDU on the LAODENG Universe network.

## Before You Begin

Make sure your wallet is connected to the official LAODENG Universe network.

| Setting | Value |
|---|---|
| Network Name | LAODENG Universe |
| RPC URL | https://rpc.laodeng.org |
| Chain ID | 20260001 |
| Currency Symbol | LDU |
| Block Explorer | https://scan.laodeng.org |

## Receive LDU

1. Open your EVM-compatible wallet.
2. Switch to **LAODENG Universe**.
3. Copy your wallet address.
4. Share only your public wallet address with the sender.
5. Wait for the transaction to be confirmed.
6. Check the transaction on the official block explorer.

Official block explorer:

`https://scan.laodeng.org`

## Transfer LDU

1. Open your wallet.
2. Switch to **LAODENG Universe**.
3. Select **Send**.
4. Enter the recipient's wallet address.
5. Enter the amount of LDU.
6. Review the address, amount, and network fee carefully.
7. Confirm the transaction.
8. Wait for the transaction to be included in a block.

## Transaction Fees

LDU is the native asset of LAODENG Universe and is used to pay network transaction fees.

Your wallet must contain enough LDU to cover both:

- The transfer amount
- The network gas fee

## Verify a Transaction

After sending LDU, copy the transaction hash and open:

`https://scan.laodeng.org`

Use the explorer to verify:

- Transaction status
- Sender address
- Recipient address
- Transferred amount
- Block number
- Network fee

## Security Warning

Always verify the recipient address before confirming a transfer.

Blockchain transactions are generally irreversible after confirmation.

Never share your private key or seed phrase with anyone.

LAODENG UNIVERSE representatives will never privately request your wallet credentials.
