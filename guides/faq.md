# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is LAODENG Universe?

LAODENG Universe is an independent EVM-compatible blockchain network powered by its native asset, LDU.

## What is LDU?

LDU is the native asset of LAODENG Universe. It is used for network transactions and gas fees.

## What is the Chain ID?

The LAODENG Universe mainnet Chain ID is:

`20260001`

Hexadecimal Chain ID:

`0x13524a1`

## What is the official RPC URL?

`https://rpc.laodeng.org`

## What is the official block explorer?

`https://scan.laodeng.org`

## Can I add the network to an EVM-compatible wallet?

Yes. Use the official network parameters published in this documentation.

## Is LDU publicly tradable?

No official liquidity pool or public trading market has been launched at this time.

Do not trust unofficial claims that LDU is available through an official exchange or liquidity pool unless confirmed through the official website.

## Where can I verify announcements?

Use:

- https://laodeng.org
- https://github.com/laodeng-universe

## Will support ask for my seed phrase?

No. LAODENG UNIVERSE representatives will never ask for your private key or seed phrase.
